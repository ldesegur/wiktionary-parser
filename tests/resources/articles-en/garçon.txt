===Etymology===
From {{etyl|frm|fr}}, from {{etyl|fro|fr}} {{m|fro|garçun||servant}}, oblique case of {{m|fro|gars}}, from {{etyl|frk|fr}} {{m|frk|*wrakjō||servant, boy}} from {{etyl|gem-pro|fr}} {{m|gem-pro|*wrakjô||exile, driven one}}, from {{etyl|ine-pro|fr}} {{m|ine-pro|*wreg-||to drive}}. Cognate with {{cog|goh|wrecheo}}, {{m|goh|recko||exile, warrior, hero}} (Modern {{cog|de|Recke}}), {{cog|osx|wrekkio||a banished person, exile, stranger}}, {{cog|ang|wreċċa||a wretch, stranger, exile}}, and perhaps to {{cog|non|rekkr||man, warrior, hero}}. More at {{l|en|wretch}}, {{l|en|wreak}}.
